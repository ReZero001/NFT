Questions

What are NFTs?
NFTs, or non-fungible tokens, are unique, digital tokens that can’t be
replaced. They’re issued on a blockchain, like Ethereum. These tokens
can represent anything digital, such as art, animated stickers, music,
videos, tweets, and more.
Why should I mint and sell my NFTs through Unveil?
With Unveil, we’ve taken the complexities out of minting NFTs and
designed our NFTs structure in a way that perfectly suits your needs as
an art photographer. For example, you can add curators or galleries to
your NFTs temporarily, and you can create NFTs with or without prints
associated with them. We also offer an exhibition like experience since
we know the story behind your work is your most valuable asset.
I already sold some print editions, can I create an NFT of these works?
Yes. When you mint your ark works as NFTs through Unveil you can
specify how many editions have been sold already, all information safely
secured in the blockchain.
Who owns the NFTs once they are created?
Unveil never owns your works. You will always be the owner of the
artworks until the moment you sell them.
If I create my NFTs at Unveil can I still work with my gallery?
Yes you can. We encourage you to do so. Just connect your gallery to
your NFTs. They do need to create a wallet for this purpose. Choose the
distribution percentage and all income is distributed directly.
Do I need to create a new website?
No, you don't. We know you've worked hard on your website and
curated Instagram, and we like to keep it that way. You will get your own
personal NFT gallery to display the NFTs you have on sale. This NFT
gallery can be easily added to your website as a widget or link to your
Instagram. We can also feature your work on Unveil - a curated selection
of the world's best NFTs by art photographers.
What do I need to pay to Unveil?
Our Forever Free plan allows you to sell NFTs without any monthly costs.
You only pay a small percentage when you actually sell an NFT. If you
need more features or don't wish to pay per transaction. You can always
upgrade to our Pro and Artist plans, which have fixed monthly costs
associated with them. For curators and gallerists, we have custom plans
developed.
